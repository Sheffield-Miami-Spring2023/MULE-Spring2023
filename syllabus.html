Course Information

Description

The Miami University Laptop Ensemble (MULE) provides students the opportunity to perform electro-acoustic and digital music using laptops, tablets, various sensors, controllers, synths, and other electronic instruments. Students will gain an understanding of how to use computers and other emerging music technologies to create music in an ensemble environment. Additionally, they will develop music industry strategies for ensemble development, such as publicity, booking, and promoting music.

Learning Outcomes

  1. Exhibit fluency in the use of the hardware and software necessary to effectively participate in live music making with electronic devices.
  2. Identify, recognize, and correct technical problems that may arise in hardware and software.
  3. Develop an awareness and ability to navigate the group dynamic of a coordinated performing ensemble.
  4. Demonstrate musical creativity during rehearsals and performances.
  5. Exhibit improved individual musicianship.

    • You are expected to perform in all required concerts and arrive at the designated concert call times.

Communication

  • You are expected to regularly check Canvas Announcements, our Discord channel, and your Miami email inbox.
  • Use email for any correspondence that warrants documentation (e.g. excused absences, grading errors or disputes, etc). Send all email messages from your miamioh.edu account.   • Use the #mule channel on the Music Comp/Tech Discord server for informal chat, quick questions, peer assistance, and brainstorming.

Sometimes, the information you need can be surprisingly easy to find, and solving small problems through self-directed research is an indispensable skill.
